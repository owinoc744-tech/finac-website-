Common Questions

Before you write...

Can I write for FINAC?

We occasionally work with contributors who have verified finance credentials. Send your pitch and a writing sample to hello@finac.com with the subject line "Contributor Pitch".

Do you offer financial advice?

Our content is educational and informational only. We are not licensed financial advisors. For personalised advice, please consult a qualified professional in your country.

How do you make money?

FINAC earns revenue through display advertising (Google AdSense). We do not accept payment for editorial content, sponsored reviews, or affiliate links without clear disclosure.

Can I republish your articles?

Our content is copyrighted. You may quote up to 100 words with a link back to the original. For full republication rights, please contact us first.

How do I unsubscribe from emails?

Every newsletter we send includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom. Click it and you'll be removed immediately — no questions asked.

I found an error in an article

We take accuracy seriously. If you spot a mistake, please let us know via this form or email hello@finac.com. We'll review and correct it promptly.
