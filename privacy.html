Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FINAC ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit finac.com. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information — your name and email address if you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, browser type, device type, and referring URLs, collected automatically via analytics tools.
  • Cookies — small text files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience and serve relevant ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver and improve our content and website experience
  • Send you our newsletter and financial tips (only if you opted in)
  • Analyse traffic and understand how readers use our site
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

FINAC uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings. You can also opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. Google Analytics collects information such as how often you visit, what pages you visit, and what other sites you used before coming to FINAC. We use this data to improve our site. Google's ability to use and share information collected by Google Analytics is restricted by the Google Analytics Terms of Service.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function properly
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand traffic and usage patterns
  • Advertising cookies — used by Google AdSense to serve relevant ads

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

6. Third-Party Links

Our articles may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

7. Newsletter & Email

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address to send you our weekly content. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email we send. We will never sell your email address to third parties.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy. Newsletter subscribers' data is retained until they unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained in accordance with Google's standard retention periods.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for email communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@finac.com.

10. Children's Privacy

FINAC is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
privacy@finac.com
